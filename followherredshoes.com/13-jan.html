Military Breakfast meetings: Military Interoperability

ME, Navy, Army, and Military Outreach

Ahhhh…..we eat….ON OUR OWN PERSONAL DIME hahaha and meet. If you work for a company, they pay for your breakfast but if you work for the military or government, you pay your own way. Interesting…  In attendance are several defense industry partners in Orlando but the showcase is of the four branches of military – particularly focused on training and education. The primary goal of this meeting is to discuss some of the challenges facing our defense training community. Of highest priority is interoperability – or the ability for computer and network based training systems to be able to share data. Why? Because a) if they can’t, then every branch and every topic area has to have it’s own training system. This is very expensive. And b) when systems can share data about a person, then each system can be individualized to meet the trainee’s needs. In other words, it will save massive money and time if systems of the future can be interoperable.
