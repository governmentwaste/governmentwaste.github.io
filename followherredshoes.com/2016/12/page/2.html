Directors’ Meeting: Global Partners

December 2, 2016 jjvogelwalcutt
Uncategorized
0 Comment

I really feel like government meeting never end….truly….never…..end….

If you have the grand notion that government workers sit around all day eating bon bons….well, I assure you, THIS government worker does not. And none of the ones I have witness do anything but work work work. This meeting is focused on global connections for education. I am SO EXCITED by the ideas flowing!! Top idea of the day: Global Ambassador Program. Let’s help students around the world study at different research programs and help improve education standards and teaching methods to improve a Global Education Model. Wouldn’t that be A-M-A-Z-I-N-G?????!!!!

The I/ITSEC Ball

December 1, 2016 jjvogelwalcutt
Uncategorized
0 Comment

I always wondered where people wore those beautiful gowns we all stare at in the movies. I wore a dress to prom – actually, my mom and I designed them and she sewed them. They were beautiful and for an evening, I felt like a teenage princess. Then I became a mom and the “early childhood” themed living room replaced dreams of dancing in pretty dresses. BUT NOW….I get to wear gowns, gowns, and more gowns!!! As a speaker at a conference like this, we get a free ticket to the ball. Fancy food, fancy clothes, photographers, and even dancing. It is a dream, it is fun, and to sit amongst folks who are world changers is……UNBELIEVABLE. : )
