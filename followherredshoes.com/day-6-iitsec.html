Global Partnernship Meet-n-Greet

Global Partnernship Meet-n-GreetNovember 27, 2016 jjvogelwalcutt
Uncategorized
0 Comment

First thing to note: THIS IS A VOLUNTARY ACTIVITY. Did you ever wonder how events are paid for when you are a government employee? Do the taxer payers pay? NO. I can’t emphasize it enough. It is often asked who pays for food? drinks? etc etc – We, the employees, pay for ourselves. If you want to eat or drink or take part in these activities – they are voluntary and you pay your OWN way. That said….it IS necessary to spend time with co-workers, even international – or probably especially international partners – in non-work focused ways. Here we were given the opportunity before the big conference begins to cook and collaborate with government researchers around the world!!! TOO MUCH FUN!!
