Revolutionizing Education

Revolutionizing EducationFebruary 21, 2017 jjvogelwalcutt
Uncategorized
0 Comment

Ever wonder what is next? Will we always teach the way we always have? As an expert in a concept called cognitive efficiency – I can tell you, efficiently, the answer is simply, no. To date, we have taught in a specific order – we call this, linear teaching. In the future, learning will be non-linear – it will be everywhere, all the time. How?

Workout before work….a MUST! 4 miles, BAM

Picts of OPM….hallways….these are all oh so boring heading to the computer help desk…

   In order to change the “learning ecosystem”, we have to first connect instructional types to one another so that students can transition from one experience to the next seamlessly. One-on-one teachers are the most effective, in most cases, for learning. But it is not always possible or cost-effective to teach in that manner all the time. So we turn to technology.

   At the Advanced Distributed Learning Initiative laboratory (my lab), we are pioneering the way forward for educational advances. Though funded under the Office of the Secretary of Defense (Personnel and Readiness), the scientists in this lab are creating the rules (or specifications) that will be used worldwide for industry, government, and academia to ensure that every learning experience you have can inform your next one.

Starbucks just outside OPM!!

When you can always be learning something new, instead of repeating work you have already completed, your knowledge base and skills will grow exponentially!!!
