About Me

Ever wonder what it’s like to join the federal government? I’m not a politician. I’m a scientist. Specifically, I’m a psychologist. I didn’t grow up in D.C. I didn’t even grow up in an educated or wealthy family. But as Dr. Suess so elegantly and simply put it…I’ve got brains in my head and feet in my shoes and I can steer myself wherever I choose. And so I did….right into civilian service. You might ask – WHY?! Why in the world would one want to do that?

Well, I’ll tell you – I believe in our country, I believe in service, and I believe in spreading good work to help others. Having worked with military personnel for the past 12 years, I’ve had the distinct pleasure to meet incredibly diligent, dedicated, and patriotic people. I can expect no less from myself.

Follow me – with a lot of class, a little bit of sass, and a whole lot of brilliance….as I take on a human innovation fellowship that allows me entrance into all aspects of the government with the primary goal of improving its efficiency….and then bringing all that I learn to the American people…..and what a time to be asked to share insider information about the US government!
